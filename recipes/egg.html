Vegetable Egg Bake

Description

Looking for a versatile breakfast recipe? Try this vegetable egg bake from A Kitchen Addiction. I think I've made it at least 30 times, and most of the time I vary the ingredients slightly depending on what's in season or in my kitchen. It comes out beautifully and keeps well in the fridge throughout the week. It's also a fantastic breakfast to serve for a crowd!

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 C fresh baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 of an orange or red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 C cherry or grape tomatoes, quartered
  • 1/4 C onion, chopped
  • 1 C shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 C shredded cheddar cheese
  • cilantro for garnish, optional

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Lightly spray a 9 x 13 inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Whisk together eggs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Mix in spinach, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, onion, and Â¾ C mozzarella.
  3. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish. Bake for 20 minutes. Carefully remove from oven and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and cheddar cheeses. Bake for an additional 20 minutes.
  4. Serve warm with cilantro, if desired. Store leftovers in the refrigerator.
